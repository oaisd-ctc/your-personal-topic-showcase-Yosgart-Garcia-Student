Goin' back

Man, Hell must suck, you think to yourself as you shamefully walk back up the convinient set of stairs
located by the entrance of the fourth circle.

You stop by gluttony to give Cerberus a pet.

Wisely reflecting upon your time in Hell, you come up with a list of reasons it sucks, presented below!

Top 5 reasons inferno sucks:
  1. It's pretty painful
  2. It's pretty ugly
  3. It's pretty smelly
  4. It's pretty nasty
  5. It's pretty loud

Wow, what an insightful list!
You pat yourself on the back.

Wishing to remember this experience more fondly, you take out your journal and write down some summaries about the layers!

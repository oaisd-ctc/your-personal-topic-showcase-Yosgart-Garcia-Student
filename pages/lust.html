Limbo

NOW THE CHOIR OF ANGUISH, LIKE A WOUND, STRIKES THROUGH THE TORTURED AIR.
NOW I HAVE COME TO HELL'S FULL LAMENTATION, SOUND BEYONG SOUND.

Here you see the souls of those who lived sinlessly but existed before Christ.
Unable to reach Heaven, they are reserved a paradise in Hell,
their only torment being the hopelessness of never being able to ascend.

You recognize the faces of great figuers such as Aristotle, Homer, and Caesar.


Descend to LUST.

The Lustful

NOW THE CHOIR OF ANGUISH, LIKE A WOUND, STRIKES THROUGH THE TORTURED AIR.
NOW I HAVE COME TO HELL'S FULL LAMENTATION, SOUND BEYOND SOUND.

The tempest of passion's crime tosses you about as you try to pass through...

In the second circle lives an eternal storm delivering poetic justice.
The sinners here exist within the winds of the storm, flying about forever,
just as in life they let themselves be carried by the strength of their desires.

Being only the second circle of Hell, you could argue that all these people did was love too much.

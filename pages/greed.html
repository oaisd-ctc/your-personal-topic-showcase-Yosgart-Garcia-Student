The Hoarders and Wasters

In the first life beneath the sun
they were so skewed and squint-eyed in their minds
their misering or extravagance mocked all reason.

The voice of each clamors its own excess
when lust meets lust at the two points of the circle
where oppoosite guilts meet in their wretchedness.



As you watch the hoarders and wasters face off for all eternity, burdened to repeat the same endless task
just as King Sisyphus. Here among the hoarders you find your fork!
This whole Hell place is pretty scary, so you want to leave immediately

And you don't want to imagine what those in the lower layers are suffering through...

Ascend back.

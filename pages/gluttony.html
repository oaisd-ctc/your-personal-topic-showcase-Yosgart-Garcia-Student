Gluttony

You find yourself in Dante's shoes, blocked from the Mount of Joy.
Using human reason, you must descend and recognize sin.

Your citizens nicknamed nme Ciacco, The Hog:
gluttony was my offense, and for it
I lie here rotting like a swollen log.

Nor am I lost in this alone; all these
you see about you in this painful death
have wallowed in the same indecencies.


You stand at the gate of Hell, being able to overhear the cries of The Opportunists.
The first river of Hell, Acheron, sits ways away from you. Charon may ferry you over, if you wish.


Be ferried to LIMBO.

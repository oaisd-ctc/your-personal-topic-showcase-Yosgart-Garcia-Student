Gluttony

Your citizens nicknamed nme Ciacco, The Hog:
gluttony was my offense, and for it
I lie here rotting like a swollen log.

Nor am I lost in this alone; all these
you see about you in this painful death
have wallowed in the same indecencies.


Here you come upon the horrible sight of Cerberus ripping and tearing apart the gluttons.
They in life wallowed in food and drinks, producing not much more than garbage.
Here, they themselves lie in the slush of their putrid excess.


Descend to GREED.
